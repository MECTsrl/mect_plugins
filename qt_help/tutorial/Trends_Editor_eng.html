Trends Editor

“Trends Editor” configures history variables (variables with S, F, V and X in the “Update” field of “Crosstable Editor”) to plot in the time domain.

In the “Trend Editor” it is possible to setup 4 traces (graph).

WARNING: the trend variables must be historicized (see chapter “History functioning”) and must have been activated by logStart() function for example within the setup(void) function of the automation.cpp file.
